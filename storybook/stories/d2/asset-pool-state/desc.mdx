


## States

 - **ACTIVE**: The pool is operational, and assets can be issued, transferred, or retired within it. Trading is allowed, and assets are actively managed.
 - **ON_HOLD**: The pool is temporarily suspended. No trading or management of assets can occur. The pool may be resumed or closed permanently.
 - **CLOSED**: The pool has been permanently closed. No further trading or management of assets is allowed. The pool cannot be reopened.
